• How much house can I afford with a conventional loan?

If you are in the same position as I am, you may be interested in buying a house at this time in your life. There are many questions that come up when deciding to buy a house such as style, location, and preferences; but the most important is finance. How much are you supposed to spend on a house? Most lenders reccommend a monthly mortgage payment of 28% of your gross monthly income for a conventional loan. Lenders also require at least 20% of the home value as a downpayment in a conventional loan purchase. There are many other factors that may affect your ability to qualify for a conventional loan such as debt and reliability of income but for simplicity sake, ignore those other factors at this time and assume you have no other significant debt. To calculate how much house you can afford, you will need to know your annual gross income, your approximate interest rate for a loan, and the loan length.
